Viewing and Downloading

Viewing and downloading photos in Viewfinder is simple:

  • Tap an image to view it full-screen, then swipe to move between photos.
  • Double-tap an image to download it to your iPad's Photos application.
  • Tap and hold an image to view additional information about it.

A note about copyright and image permissions

Viewfinder respects the permissions set by the photographer on Flickr, so be sure to check that the photo's license permits its intended use - the ability to download an image is not necessarily an indication of your right to use it.

If you use a Creative Commons-licensed photo, you're required to credit the photographer. Tap Next to find out how Viewfinder helps ensure the license-relevant credit when using a photo.
