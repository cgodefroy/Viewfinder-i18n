Copying Links to Photos

Viewfinder keeps a history of your image downloads so you can easily come back and copy the relevant URLs and attribution for images. If you're using a Creative Commons license be sure you link and attribute as required.

  • Tap and hold on a photo to reveal the Info popover
  • Use the Info popover to copy the relevant Flickr link and image attribution.

This information is also available from the History tab of the Downloads popover.

If you're ever unsure of the license for an image, you can use the Info popover to view a short description of the license terms.
