Filtering by License

To help you with your image research, Viewfinder lets you restrict your search to photos which have specific Creative Commons licenses (such as a license permitting commercial use).

Tap the Info button beside the search field to select a Creative Commons license. You can further restrict the search to photos that are licensed for commercial purposes, as well as those which allow you to create derivative works.

Find out more about Creative Commons licenses at creativecommons.org
